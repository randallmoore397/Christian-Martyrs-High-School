About us

Brief History of Christian Martyrs

The Christian Martyrs High School was established by the Church of God Liberia Inc. in 1999, July. The first principal was Mr. Augustus Yormeh, and he was successful in running the school till 2001. Mr. Augustus was succeed by Mr. Jesssie B. Williams who took over as the second principal in the year 2002 where he serves as principal up to 2017.

Under his administration the Christian Martyrs School was elevated to the senior high level. 2015, Mr. Williams and the Church board deemed it necessary in adding 12th grade to the school. 2016, The Christian Martyrs High School send her first batch of 12th graders for the West African Certificate Exam, and she came out with 100%, and were honored by the Ministry of Education in agreement with the President of Liberia at that time, Ex-president Madam Ellen Johnson Sirleaf. In 2017, Mr. Williams was asked to turn over the institution as principal; however, he was succeed by Mr. John M. Folokuleh who is now the current principal of the Christian Martyrs High School.

Our school run from Kindergarten (ABC) to Senior High (12th) Division. We run only morning section which comprises of both Academic classes and Trade Classes.
