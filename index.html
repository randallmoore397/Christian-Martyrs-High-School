About us

Welcome to Christian Martyrs School

Christian Martyrs High School was established by the Church of God Liberia Inc. in July, 1999. We are located at Phebe Grey Mission Compound, Schiefflin Township, Robertfield Highway, Margibi County. Our school run from Kindergarten (ABC) to Senior High (12th) Division. We run only morning section which comprises of both Academic classes and Trade Classes.

As a new student, you are more then welcome to experience the quality eduction, and life-changing academic and Trade classes offered at CMHS.

Mr. John M. Folokuleh is the current principal of the Christian Martyrs High School.

Catering

Catering students will enjoy a diverse and exciting learning experience, exploring the art of culinary creativity and hospitality. From mastering culinary techniques to understanding menu planning, food presentation, and event management, they will acquire valuable skills to excel in the dynamic world of catering and hospitality services. With opportunities to work in various settings such as restaurants, hotels, and event planning companies, catering students will gain exposure to the culinary world while satisfying their passion for creating delightful and memorable dining experiences for customers. .
